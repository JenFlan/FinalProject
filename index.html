Global Site Visit

The easiest way to plan professional trips for international educators worldwide

Global Site Visit is a tool for international educators worldwide to easily map travel, track important details about each portion of the trip, find helpful tools for evaluating your visits, and easily create trip reports.

Planning and executing a successful site visit, conference trip or visit to a research partner is complicated. A travel agent can book your air ticket, but organizing the many goals, logistics, contact details and meeting agendas takes a lot of work and coordinating the best sequence of cities and dates can take a lot of time.

Good planning has been shown to reduce cost by 17% and improve outcomes by 8%. Global Site Visithelps you easily organize your trip to maximize your time, budget, and most importantly, improve the effectiveness of your partnerships.

You can use Global Site Visit for:

  • Planning travel to conferences and meetings at the conference
  • Maximize your time abroad by efficiently planning visits to multiple partners and conferences
  • Measure and report the success of your trips
  • Helps you gather important details for evaluating the effectiveness of your partnerships
  • Easily generate your itinerary to give to your travel agent
